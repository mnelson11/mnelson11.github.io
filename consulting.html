(Winter 2016)

Project | 2

Fungicide Market Analysis
(Fall 2016)

I worked in a group of five with the NC agricultural company, AgBiome, in preparation for their release of a new biological fungicide. My group explored the competitor landscape for fungicides, as well as researched organic crop acreage in the United States in order to present AgBiome with a recommendation of where to focus their marketing. We also prepared spreadsheets for their use containing fungicide competitor brand information, and acreage details for the most abundant organic crops in the United States.
